About

Robert has been hooked on magic since the day his 4 th grade teacher performed a magic show for the class.  Mr. Spears, who happened to be an amateur magician, quickly became Robert’s inspiration, taught the class how to do the cups and balls routine during that show.  Since that time, Robert was on and off with magic until his late 20’s where by chance, he stumbled upon a class that taught magic. This rekindled the fire in the wide-eyed 4 th grader in him and has kept him active with learning and performing ever since. Robert’s specialty is close-up card tricks but has studied many other types of the performing art. He has been a member of IBM (International Brotherhood of Magicians) for 15 years and SCAM (Southern California Association of Magicians) for 14 years. If you are interested in a show or lessons, he can be reached at robert@quandini.com.
